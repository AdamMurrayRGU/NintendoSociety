FAQ

How old is Nintendo?

Nintendo, was founded on the 23rd of September, 1889. Nintendo was originally a toy manufacturer company in Japan founded by Fusajiro Yamauchi.


When was the nintendo society formed?

The Nintendo Society was originally formed on the 6th of October, 2020. It was founded by the Nintendo boyz, a team that consists of Connor Mackay, Neil, Alex McNaughton, Adam Murray, and Norvin Razario.


What is the point of the Nintendo Society?

The main reason for the Nintendo Society's existence was part of the CM1101 course, because we needed to make a website for our chosen society and we chose to make a society about Nintendo. But other than that it's a place for Nintendo fans to gather and appreciate all the memories they have given us.


How can I ask a question?

Asking a question is simple, you just have to fill out the little form at the bottom of the page, and press the submit button, then a member of our team will answer your question. (if possible)


Why Nintendo?

The main reason why we chose to make our website about a Nintendo Society was because everyone in our group was a fan of nintendo anyway, do it just made sense to do out project on something we all have a common intrest for, and that we already know a little bit about.
